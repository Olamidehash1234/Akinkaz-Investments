About Us

Who
We Are

Precision-driven engineering across Nigeria

Akinkaz Investment Limited is a premier engineering and investment solutions company delivering reliable EPC services and upstream financing to clients within the oil & gas and industrial sectors.

With over eight years of hands-on experience, we have built a strong reputation for integrity, project excellence, and timely delivery. As a proudly Nigeria-based company, we combine financial strength, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of the local operating environment to support operators, investors, and engineering projects with confidence.

Our Mission

To empower clients with reliable engineering and financing solutions that drive growth, enhance productivity, and ensure sustainable project success.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted partner in EPC services and upstream oil & gas financing.
